FAQ's!

Welcome to our FAQ page, where we aim to provide clear answers to the most commonly asked questions about our legal services. We understand that navigating the legal landscape can be complex and overwhelming, and that's why we have compiled this comprehensive list of frequently asked questions (FAQs). By addressing inquiries about fees, the legal process, and other relevant topics, we hope to save both potential clients and ourselves valuable time.

How are legal fees determined?



At our firm, we offer various fee structures depending on the nature of the case. We may work on a contingency basis, where the fee is a percentage of the final settlement or verdict. Alternatively, we may charge an hourly rate or provide a fixed fee arrangement. During your initial consultation, we will discuss the fee structure that best suits your case and provide transparent information about costs.

What is the legal process like?



The legal process can vary depending on the type of case and jurisdiction. Generally, it involves several stages, including investigation, filing legal documents, discovery, negotiations, and, if necessary, trial. As your attorney, I will guide you through each step, ensuring you understand the process and your rights at every stage. Rest assured, I will work diligently to achieve the best possible outcome for your case.

How long will my case take to resolve?



The duration of a case depends on several factors, such as the complexity of the legal issues involved, the cooperation of all parties, and court availability. Some cases can be resolved relatively quickly through negotiations or settlements, while others may require more time for thorough investigation and litigation. During our initial consultation, I will assess the specifics of your case and provide an estimate of the anticipated timeline.

Will my information be kept confidential?



Yes, absolutely. Client confidentiality is of utmost importance to us. As an attorney, I am bound by professional ethics and legal obligations to maintain the confidentiality of all client information. Rest assured that any information shared with us will be treated with the strictest confidentiality and will only be used for the purpose of your legal representation.

Do you handle cases outside of Albuquerque?



While our main office is located in Albuquerque, we handle cases not only within the local jurisdiction but also in various regions. We have experience in navigating different jurisdictions and can represent clients in multiple locations, depending on the nature of the case. Please reach out to us to discuss your specific situation, and we will determine how best to assist you.
